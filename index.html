This is Ticker Board


A tabletop, stock ticker display that shows you the changes in the market second by second for stocks that YOU CHOOSE

It is a fun and useful tool that you can use to avoid straying away from your work to check the market

Who Are We?

TickerBoard is a group of University of Michigan students. We want to build a product that many people can enjoy. TickerBoard is simply a class project that we hope has potential to live past the course. Contact Us

TickerBoard is a simple machine, that does a simple, yet powerful, service
