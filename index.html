A tabletop, stock ticker display that shows changes in the market second by second, personalized to the companies that you care about! TickerBoard is a fun and useful tool that comes in a variety of sizes.

Who Are We?

TickerBoard is a group of University of Michigan students. We want to build a product that many people can enjoy. TickerBoard is simply a class project that we hope has potential to live past the course.
